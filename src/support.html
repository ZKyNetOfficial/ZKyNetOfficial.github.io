Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about ZKyNet's privacy infrastructure

What makes ZKyNet different from traditional VPNs?

ZKyNet combines Tor-style anonymity with legal compliance through Zero-Knowledge proofs. Unlike traditional VPNs that rely on trust, our architecture provides cryptographic guarantees while meeting enterprise compliance requirements.

How does Zero-Knowledge compliance work?

Exit nodes provide cryptographic proofs of legitimacy without revealing specific user activities. This enables abuse mitigation and legal compliance while preserving user privacy through generic activity indicators.

When will ZKyNet products be available?

We're currently in development with plans to launch consumer and enterprise solutions in 2025. Our MVP VPN demonstrates our technical capabilities and is available for testing.

Is ZKyNet open source?

Yes, ZKyNet is committed to open-source development. Our code is available on GitHub, and we welcome community contributions to advance privacy infrastructure technology.

How can I get involved or contribute?

You can contribute through our GitHub repositories, join our community discussions on Reddit, or contact us for partnership opportunities. We're always looking for developers, researchers, and privacy advocates.
