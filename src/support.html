Frequently Asked Questions

ZKyNet is a tunnel management platform. It ships with its own encrypted tunnel infrastructure so it works out of the box, and it lets you import and manage tunnels from any source. Your providers, your servers, or ours. Think of it as one place to manage all your encrypted connections.

If you have .conf (WireGuard) or .ovpn (OpenVPN) files, yes. Import them into ZKyNet and it handles the routing, DNS, and tunnel lifecycle. Your configs work regardless of which provider or server issued them.

ZKyNet is launching on Linux, macOS, Windows, and Android. The desktop version ships as a dedicated browser client with a bundled system daemon. The system daemon handles tunnels independently, so tunnels stay active even when the browser is closed.

ZKyNet operates at the application layer inside the browser, where domain information is visible. You set rules like "route banking.com through a direct connection" or "route streaming.example.com through tunnel B" using a visual interface. The system daemon applies per-app routing at the OS level, and the browser client handles per-domain decisions.
